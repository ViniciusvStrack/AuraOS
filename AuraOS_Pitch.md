# Pitch Deck: AuraOS

## The Problem
Corporate amnesia. Companies spend billions hiring talent, but 40% of their productivity is lost to "reinventing the wheel" because information is siloed in people's heads or buried in thousands of Slack messages.

## The Solution
AuraOS: The first **Cognitive Operating System**. 
It's not a tool you use; it's the intelligence layer that connects your existing tools.

## Market Size
- Target: Mid-to-Large Enterprises (100+ employees).
- Total Addressable Market: $250B+ (Knowledge Management & AI Productivity market).

## Revenue Model
- **Core:** $20/user/month.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing with local LLM deployment for high security.
- **Upsell:** "Aura Insights" for HR/Management to identify burnout and talent gaps.

## Innovation Stacks
1. **Neural Tribal Knowledge:** Captures "why" decisions were made, not just "what".
2. **Proactive Intervention:** The AI interrupts workflows to prevent documented mistakes.
3. **Identity-Aware Privacy:** Automatically filters sensitive info based on corporate hierarchy.
